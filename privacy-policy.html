Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

Nearby Finder ("the App") is a free mobile application that helps users discover nearby places of interest — including petrol pumps, temples, washrooms, parking areas, metro stations, hospitals, ATMs, and restaurants. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Location Data

The App requests access to your device's precise location (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION) only while you are actively using the App ("foreground" access). Your location is used solely to find nearby places and calculate driving distances. We do not store, log, or transmit your location to our own servers. Location coordinates are sent directly from your device to third-party APIs (listed below) to perform the search.

Recent Searches

The App stores your last 5 search locations locally on your device using device storage (AsyncStorage). This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can clear it at any time using the "Clear" button in the app.

Theme Preference

Your chosen display theme (light or dark mode) is saved locally on your device. This preference is not transmitted anywhere.

No Account or Personal Data

The App does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal identifier.

No Analytics or Advertising

We do not use any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting tools that collect personal data.

2. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with the following third-party services to function:

Service Purpose Data Sent Privacy Policy
Google Places API Find nearby places (petrol pumps, hospitals, restaurants, etc.) and provide address autocomplete suggestions Your location coordinates and search text Google Privacy Policy
Google Geocoding API Convert a selected address or landmark into geographic coordinates Selected place ID or address text Google Privacy Policy
Google Maps (deep link) Open navigation when you tap "Open in Google Maps" Destination coordinates only (handled by the Google Maps app on your device) Google Privacy Policy
OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine) Calculate driving distance and estimated travel time to each place Origin and destination coordinates OSRM Project
Overpass API / OpenStreetMap (fallback) Used as a fallback data source when Google Places is unavailable Your location coordinates OSM Privacy Policy

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To find places of interest within 5 km of your current or searched location.
  • To calculate driving distance and estimated travel time to each place.
  • To show address suggestions as you type a location.
  • To remember your last 5 searches locally for your convenience.
  • To remember your display theme preference locally.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond the above.

4. Data Storage and Retention

The App does not operate any backend server or database. All app data (recent searches, theme preference) is stored exclusively on your device and is removed when you uninstall the App. No location data or search history is retained by us.

5. Data Safety (Google Play)

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:

  • Location (approximate and precise): Collected, used in-app only, not shared with third parties beyond the APIs listed above, not stored on our servers.
  • Search history: Stored locally on-device only. Not shared or transmitted.
  • No data is sold to third parties.
  • No personal data (name, email, phone, identifiers) is collected.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Rights

You can manage your data at any time:

  • Revoke location permission: Settings → Apps → Nearby Finder → Permissions. The App will still work for manual location search without the permission.
  • Clear recent searches: Tap "Clear" next to the Recent Searches section in the App.
  • Delete all local data: Uninstall the App from your device.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
